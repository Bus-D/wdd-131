Headings should:

Provide structure and navigation points

Be in a hieracrchial, logical order

Provide an outline for the content

Break the content into logical chunks
Be descriptive top the content they contain

Heading 1: Main content description/title Heading 2: Sub-section of Heading 1 (broad main idea) Heading 3: Sub-section of Heading 2 (specific idea) Heading 4: Sub-section of Heading 3 (examples, details) Heading 2: Next broad main idea
